# 🪪 Business & promo cards

## 💡 Concept

**Agent skill.** Produces visiting cards, flyers, and social promo images at the right size for print or platforms—with
correct margins so text is not cut off after upload or printing.

## 👤 How the user experiences it

1. The user chooses the format: business card, flyer, Instagram post, story, and similar presets.
2. They enter name, contacts, offer text, and upload a logo; they describe the visual style.
3. The agent shows one or two layout options that respect safe zones and bleed for print.
4. The user picks a layout and downloads a file ready to **print** or **post online**.
