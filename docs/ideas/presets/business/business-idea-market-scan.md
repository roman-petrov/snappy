# 💡 Business idea + market scan

## 💡 Concept

**Agent skill with web research.** From a niche or problem, the agent proposes several product or business ideas and
enriches them with a short market picture: who else plays in the space, trends, risks, and draft positioning lines. A
structured memo to react to—not financial or legal advice.

## 👤 How the user experiences it

1. The user describes the niche, customer, and constraints (solo founder, budget, region).
2. The agent returns a memo with a few **idea cards**, a competitive landscape, trends, risks, and sample taglines.
3. The user picks one idea and asks for a deeper pass on that option.
4. They export or save the memo to discuss with co-founders or mentors.
