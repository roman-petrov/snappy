# 🎞️ Client-side slideshow video

## 💡 Concept

**In-browser tool** (agent can help with order and captions). The user’s photos and music stay on the device; Snappy
assembles a slideshow video locally—no upload of personal albums to the server.

## 👤 How the user experiences it

1. The user adds photos in order and optionally a music track.
2. They set how long each slide shows and whether one slide fades into the next.
3. A progress bar runs while the video is built in the browser.
4. The user downloads **MP4** or **WebM** to share in messengers or social networks.
