When an AI request is performed, a fee for that request is charged from the balance. The charge equals the actual cost of the AI provider call via AiTunnel (base cost), increased by the Provider’s remuneration of 25%. The result is rounded to kopecks.

Current AiTunnel models and prices are published at aitunnel.ru/models and are a guide. The charge is the actual cost of the specific AiTunnel request, increased by 25%.

Charges apply to requests that were actually performed. If the balance is zero or insufficient, the relevant features may be restricted until the balance is topped up.
