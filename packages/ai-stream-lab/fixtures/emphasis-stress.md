# Emphasis stress

## Tight markers

Start **bold** then _italic_ then **again** with short gaps.

Mix `code` beside **bold** and _italic_ in one line: **a** _b_ `c` **d**.

Add ~~strike~~ next to the others: ~~x~~ **y** _z_ `w`.

Unfinished-looking edges for the cutter: prefix **suffix and also _tail and wrap.

## Nested feel

Outer **bold with _inner italic_ still bold** then plain.

Strike wrapping code: ~~gone `id` gone~~ then plain.

Double ticks and stars nearby: **x** `y` **z** _w_ ~~v~~.

## Closing

End on purpose with dense markup: **one** _two_ **three** `four` ~~five~~ **six**.
